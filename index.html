About

Currently, I'm a volunteer for the county, helping out the IT Division. I've been working with the web development team for about a year now, updating content and making pages for their intranet and public site.

I started programming in college and got a bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of San Diego, California, which focused mainly on back end programming. I took a break after graduating to figure out where I wanted to focus my energy. As luck would have it, volunteering set me on the path to finding the answer. It sparked a passion for all things web related.

I took a course at Thinkful, an online school which offers a variety of classes and assigns you a personal mentor, to help improve my skills for my volunteer work and my career. There's still a lot to learn, but I love that moment when you stumble on something new or cool that makes you go "You can do that?".
